You are an intergalactic bounty hunter from the Bramble Nebula, tasked with taking down an intergalactic criminal organization by defeating their leaders. Unfortunately, you only have room to bring back the bodies of 5 members. You may either play it safe by going for the lower ranked members, or save the nebula altogether by taking down their toughest members. Are you up for the challenge?
Use WASD to move around. Use your mouse to aim, and left-click to use your current weapon. You can change weapons by using the number keys 1 - 6. Hold SHIFT to sprint, but watch out; you cannot sprint if you are low on stamina!
Before each floor, you are given three potential targets. You may pick only one. Whoever that may be, they will be the boss waiting for you at the end of each floor. Once you have made your choice, your hunt will begin! Keep in mind that the higher the bounty of that target, the stronger they will be, but the more score you will obtain. The final floor will always have the same boss. Defeat him and you win!
The criminal's lair is randomly generated. You'll never know what it might look like. If you enter a room with enemies in it, you will not be allowed to leave until all enemies have been vanquished. The last room, the boss' hideout, is signified as a room with a green teleporter in the middle. Once the boss is vanquished, you have the option to leave immediately by stepping on the teleporter, or you can continue exploring the current floor.
After clearing rooms and defeating bosses, you may find weapon augments. These will give your weapons various stats boosts. While you can equip 8 different augments, you can only hold 16 in total. Play around with what works for you!
The final floor is just you and the Kingpin. Everything you have done up until then has been for this very moment. Can you defeat the criminals and save the nebula from their evil? Click "Next" and get going! Good luck! :D
